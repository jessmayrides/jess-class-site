Jess's Passion

Music: Exploring, Playlist Creating, and Vinyl Collecting

One of the things that I am most passionate about is music. I enjoy all types of music, but my favorite genres are pop punk and indie rock. I have been going to shows since I was 16, and to this day I've been to about 20 shows. I am always in search of new music, I am rarely satisfied and I love discovering new underground artists!

Here are a few of my favorite bands/artists:

  • Joyce Manor
  • Wolf Alice
  • Heart Attack Man
  • Taylor Swift
  • Phoebe Bridgers
  • Free Cake for Every Creature
  • Big Thief

I have a pop punk/ punk genre playlist that is over 21 hours long, and my indie genre playlist is about 35 hours long (what can I say, I love making big playlists). I also enjoy making many (perhaps too many) smaller playlists, whether it's to convey a certain feeling, or to group together songs that I think just sound nice together.
